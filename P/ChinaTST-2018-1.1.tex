(2018 China TST Test 1 #1) Let $p,q$ be positive reals with sum $1$. Show that for any $n$-tuple of reals $\left(y_1,y_2,\ldots,y_n\right)$, there exists an $n$-tuple of reals $\left(x_1,x_2,\ldots,x_n\right)$ satisfying \[p\cdot\max\left\{x_i,x_{i+1}\right\}+q\cdot\min\left\{x_i,x_{i+1}\right\}=y_i\] for all $i=1,2,\ldots,2017$, where $x_{2018}=x_1$.
